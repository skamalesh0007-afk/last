About the Department

The Department of Computer Science at Global Tech University is at the forefront of digital innovation. We focus on providing a strong theoretical foundation balanced with practical exposure to emerging technologies like AI, Blockchain, and Cybersecurity.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Science: A 4-year undergraduate program focusing on software engineering, algorithms, and data structures.
  • M.Sc. in Artificial Intelligence: A postgraduate degree exploring machine learning, neural networks, and deep learning.
  • Ph.D. in Cybersecurity: Advanced research program focused on network security and ethical hacking.
